  • About Us

    TSAMY / წამი

    Welcome to TSAMY Eyewear, where quality meets style. Founded in 2024. TSAMY is a Georgian eyewear brand dedicated to crafting exceptional eyewear using the finest materials and cutting-edge technology. At TSAMY, we pride ourselves on our commitment to quality and innovation. Each pair of TSAMY glasses is meticulously designed and crafted to provide unparalleled comfort, durability, and style. Our frames are made from lightweight titanium, ensuring a perfect balance of strength and sophistication. We believe that clarity and protection are paramount when it comes to eyewear. That's why all TSAMY lenses are of the highest quality, featuring advanced polarization technology to reduce glare and enhance visual clarity in any environment. Our mission at TSAMY is simple: to provide our customers with exceptional eyewear that enhances their everyday experiences. Why Choose TSAMY? - Premium quality eyewear crafted from lightweight titanium. - Highest quality polarized lenses for superior clarity and glare reduction. - Georgian-owned and operated, supporting local artisans and businesses. - Commitment to excellence in design, comfort, and durability. Join us in celebrating Georgian craftsmanship with TSAMY Eyewear.
